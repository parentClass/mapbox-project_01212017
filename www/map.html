FDT-LRC Building (Filemon D. Tanchoco Learning Resource Center)


The FDT-LRC is a hub of information built to provide students with multiple resources in their respective fields. It houses different libraries for each level and an Internet Laboratory for online resources. It also has the Activity center in the ground floor where small events may take place and it also houses the spiral Helix auditorium where special events are held. The building was built on 2010.

FDT (Filemon D. Tanchoco)


The FDT building (Filemon D. Tanchoco), houses the College of Arts and Sciences, College of Nursing, College of Physical Therapy, and some facilities for the College of Medicine. It was named after Filemon D. Tanchoco, the first founder of Manila Central University. Its natural ambience gives off the perfect studying atmosphere for the involved colleges. It can still hold normal general academic subjects too however. It is one of the first MCU buildings, this was built on 1936.

CBT Building (College of Business and Technology)


The College of Business Administration building was inaugurated to provide Business courses in MCU. It also houses the computer laboratories to hold technology-based classes. Today, some refer to it now as CBT building or College of Business and Technology building because two different colleges (College of Business Administration and College of Computer Studies) were fused into one. It was built on 1946.

Highschool/Elementary Department


The High school building is where the Basic Education department resides. They have their own field for holding events and activities but they can also use the University services to attend and hold events. They are right beside the Elementary department. The department was established around 1940s.
